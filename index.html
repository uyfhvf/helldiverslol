Game Introduction

Helldivers 2 is a third person shooter game developed by Arrow Studios and published by Sony. It is a sequel to Helldivers in 2015. The game is set in the era of human Daewoo Navigation, where players take on the role of helldiver submarines and drive spacecraft to various planets to carry out military missions.

The game supports online cooperation among four players, and players can call for various forms of support, such as airdropped weapons and equipment, airdropped mecha, air raids, shelling, etc. The strategic points obtained by players completing tasks will affect the course of the battle, and global players will share the overall progress of the battle.

Game Race

Terminids

The Terminids are the successors of the bugs of the First Galactic War. After years of selective breeding changed their physiology , they have escaped their Element-710 farms and now spread unchecked.

Automatons

The Automatons are the children of the Cyborgs, sharing their goal of declaring independence from Super Earth. They attack with heavily armored but slow moving infantry and vehicles, supported by fragile light infantry. They also make use of numerous support structures, such as mortars and scanning towers.

Illuminate

The Illuminate are an ancient race from the First Galactic War, once thought completely decimated after their defeat in the First Galactic War. Now, they have returned to avenge Super Earths genocide of their ancestors.

game company

Helldivers 2 is developed by Swedish game development company Arrowhead Game Studios and released by Sony. Arrowhead was founded in 2008 as just four young men developing games in the kitchen, and has now become a world-class game development studio.

In 2025, Tencent purchased over 15% of Arrowhead's equity for approximately 5 billion Swedish kronor (approximately 3.7 billion RMB), opening a new window into the Chinese market.
